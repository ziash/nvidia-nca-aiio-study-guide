## What is the NCA-AIIO?

The **NVIDIA Certified Associate: AI Infrastructure and Operations** exam validates that you can deploy, manage, and optimize GPU-accelerated AI infrastructure at the enterprise scale. It is vendor-issued, proctored online, and recognized across hyperscalers, system integrators, and enterprise IT teams building AI data centers.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Questions** | 50 |
| **Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Fee** | $125 USD |
| **Validity** | 2 years |
| **Format** | Online proctored (Pearson VUE) |

## Three exam domains

```
38% ████████████████░░░░░░░░░░  Domain 1 — Essential AI Knowledge
40% ████████████████████░░░░░░  Domain 2 — AI Infrastructure

```

### [Domain 1 — Essential AI Knowledge (38%)](01-essential-ai-knowledge/)
NVIDIA software stack · Training vs inference · AI/ML/DL fundamentals · Adoption drivers · Use cases · GPU vs CPU · AI lifecycle

### [Domain 2 — AI Infrastructure (40%)](02-ai-infrastructure/)
Hardware requirements · GPU scaling (NVLink/NVSwitch) · Power & cooling · On-prem vs cloud · Cluster components · Networking (InfiniBand/RoCE/Ethernet) · DPU & BlueField · Storage

### [Domain 3 — AI Operations (22%)](03-ai-operations/)
Data center management · Orchestration (Kubernetes/Slurm) · GPU monitoring (DCGM) · Virtualization (MIG/vGPU)
