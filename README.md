## Exam at a glance

- **Cert:** NVIDIA Certified Associate: AI Infrastructure and Operations (NCA-AIIO)
- **Format:** 50 questions · 60 minutes · online proctored
- **Fee:** $125 USD
- **Validity:** 2 years
- **Domains:** Essential AI Knowledge (38%) · AI Infrastructure (40%) · AI Operations (22%)
